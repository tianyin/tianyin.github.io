Bio in 100 words
 

Tianyin Xu is a PhD student in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University of California San Diego (UCSD). Before joining UCSD, he worked as a research staff in University of Goettingen, Germany. He received his B.Sc. and M.Eng. Degrees in Computer Science from Nanjing University, China in 2007 and 2010, respectively. His research interests lie broadly in computer systems, with the goal of making today's systems more dependable and manageable.
